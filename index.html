BAI Assist

Drafts summaries, emails, and internal communications for admin workflows.

Best for

Emails, memos, meeting summaries, and short leadership-ready updates.

How to use

  1. Paste your notes or the email thread.
  2. Ask for a summary + draft response.
  3. Specify tone (formal/neutral) and length.
  4. Review for accuracy and remove sensitive info.

Example prompts

  • “Turn these bullets into a 1-page memo for leadership.”
  • “Draft a reply acknowledging receipt and next steps.”
  • “Rewrite this to be clearer and shorter.”

Procurement Agent

Supports purchasing workflows, RFQs/RFPs, and vendor communications.

Best for

Drafting RFQ/RFP sections, vendor email templates, and compliant language outlines.

How to use

  1. Describe what you’re buying and the scope.
  2. Ask for required sections and evaluation criteria.
  3. Request vendor Q&A templates and communication drafts.
  4. Route final language through procurement/legal review.
